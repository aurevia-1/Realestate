About Sahar Zaid Ur Rehman

With an international career across Saudi Arabia, Pakistan, and the UAE, Sahar brings a powerful combination of real estate expertise, HR leadership, and global insight to Eagle & Anchor Real Estate.

She held key positions at Nesma & Partners and King Faisal Specialist Hospital in Jeddah, before moving into diplomatic administrative roles with the Saudi Consulate in Karachi. Her experience uniquely blends precision, leadership, and professionalism.

In Dubai, she now leads operations and client strategy for Eagle & Anchor, with a strong focus on personalized service, investment guidance, and long-term relationship building.
